## VLAN Configuration

The enterprise network is segmented into five VLANs:

* VLAN 10 – Management
* VLAN 20 – HR
* VLAN 30 – IT
* VLAN 40 – Sales
* VLAN 50 – Server

## Trunk Links

Trunk links were configured between the core switch and access switches to allow VLAN traffic to traverse the network infrastructure.

* Core-SW1 ↔ Access-SW2
* Core-SW1 ↔ Access-SW3
